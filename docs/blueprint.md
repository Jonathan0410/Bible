# **App Name**: Tenglish Bible Reader

## Core Features:

- Content Index: Browse the Old and New Testaments with books in Biblical order.
- Alphabetical Index: Quickly navigate to any book via an alphabetical listing.
- Bible Reader: Read the Tenglish Bible with verse numbers, clean spacing, and serif font.
- Swipe Navigation: Move between chapters with a simple swipe gesture.
- Theme Support: Switch between light and dark themes for comfortable reading.
- Font Size Control: Adjust the font size to small, medium, large, or extra large for optimal readability.
- Preference Persistence: Automatically persist settings such as last read location, language, theme, and font size.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: Soft off-white (#F8F8F2) for light theme, and deep dark gray (#121212) for dark theme, ensuring comfortable reading in any environment.
- Primary color: Royal purple (#7851A9) to bring a sense of elegance and devotion, fitting the app's purpose.
- Accent color: Muted violet (#A080C1), is analogous to the primary, creating subtle contrast for interactive elements without harshness.
- Body and headline font: 'Literata', a transitional serif, selected for its literary and vintage feel to enhance the devotional reading experience.
- Elegant gradient app bar using purple and violet tones to match professional Bible apps, ensuring a calm and devotional feel with consistent spacing and alignment.
